❤ BACKGROUND OF WARISAN COOKIES ❤


For your information, we plan to set up a company to provide cookies to customers in 2022.

Our company as a cookie provider will ensure that all supplies on Hari Raya or other days

will always have stock for distribute to wholesalers or stores. Our company name is “Warisan

Cookies”.“Warisan” is used to convey hereditary meaning. We provide different types of

cookies according to the menu. This is to ensure that the culture of our society is always

remembered. There are several types of cookies that we serve. For examples, London almonds,

Semperit, cornflakes, coco cookies and more.



In addition, we use the concept “We don’t make it till you order it”. Therefore, our company

staff will cook the cookies when get reservation and will distributing them at customer’s request.

Besides, this is can open the job opportunities for the community to serve in Malay sector. This

is can ensure the quality of product will guaranteed from all aspect and Halal. The location

we choose for established our company is in the area of Marang, Terengganu. Marang is capital for Kuala Terengganu.

Marang provided many facilities. This facility can help in simplifying the all process happen

in the company. In addition, the opportunities to expanding the distribution area for raising

the name of the product and service offered will be wide open. Thus, Warisan Cookies business

cac grow with successful.
